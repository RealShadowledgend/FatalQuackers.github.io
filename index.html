Deep Dive & Explanations

What's included in Building?
Assets & Props

Includes modeling and texturing for everything from simple background clutter (crates) to highly specific, detailed hero assets (furniture, weapons).

Structures

Ranges from decorative shop facades (Exterior) to fully fleshed-out, playable buildings with designed rooms and structural lighting (Interior).

What's included in Animation?
R6 vs. R15 Rigs

R6 is priced lower due to fewer joints. R15 requires complex keyframing for hands, feet, and torso bends to look natural.

Combat & Sets

From single heavy sword strikes, all the way to complex 3-hit M1 combos or cinematic sequences requiring specific timing.
