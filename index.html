How the Process Works

01

Consultation

We discuss your project scale. Once a price is agreed upon, a 25% security deposit is required to begin.

02

Development

I start building/animating. You will receive regular watermarked screenshots or video updates of the progress.

03

Revisions

You review the final product. You are entitled to two free minor revisions to make sure it's perfect.

04

Handoff

The final 75% payment (plus 30% tax if applicable) is sent. Once confirmed, the .rbxm files are delivered instantly.

A Note on Percentages (%): For established studios or highly promising projects, I am willing to waive the upfront fees in exchange for a negotiated percentage split of the game's revenue. Contact me to discuss!
