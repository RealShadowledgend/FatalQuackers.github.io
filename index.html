Deep Dive & Explanations

What's included in Building?
Assets & Props

Includes modeling and texturing for everything from simple background clutter (crates, foliage) to highly specific, detailed hero assets (furniture, weapons, vehicles).

Structures

Ranges from decorative shop facades (Exterior Only) to fully fleshed-out, playable buildings with designed rooms, stairs, and structural lighting (With Interior).

Map Environments

Small maps cover enclosed 1v1 arenas or basic simulator lobbies. Large maps cover vast open worlds, detailed cityscapes, and terrain modeling requiring extensive time to optimize properly.

What's included in Animation?
R6 vs. R15 Rigs

R6 animations are priced on the lower end due to fewer joints. R15 animations are slightly higher as they require more complex keyframing for hands, feet, and torso bends to look natural.

Loops & Emotes

Perfectly transitioning idles, walks, runs, and sit animations. Emotes involve 5-10 seconds of expressive, continuous movement tailored to a specific mood.

Combat & Action Sets

From single heavy sword strikes and gun reloads, all the way to complex 3-hit M1 combos or cinematic sequences that require specific timing and weight behind the movement.

Payment Structures

We can negotiate a payment structure that builds trust.
*(Note: You must cover the 30% Roblox Tax if paying via Gamepass/Shirts)*

100% Upfront

The safest route for me. You pay the full amount before development begins, which guarantees your spot at the top of my priority list and ensures rapid delivery.

50 / 50 Split

The most common route. You pay 50% as a secure deposit. Once the work is finished and previewed, you pay the remaining 50% for the files.

Pay After

Reserved for trusted clients and verified studios. I complete the work entirely, show you the final product in a locked showcase, and you pay prior to final handoff.
